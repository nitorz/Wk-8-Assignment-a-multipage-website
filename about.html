Our Story

LearnForAll was born from a simple but powerful realization: traditional educational technology wasn't serving everyone. In 2019, our founder Kenneth Francis, while working with students with disabilities, witnessed firsthand how technology barriers prevented brilliant minds from accessing quality education.

What started as a small research project at Zetech University quickly grew into a mission-driven platform. We partnered with disability advocacy groups, special education teachers, and students themselves to understand the real challenges and build solutions that truly work.

We're not just building software ‚Äì we're building a more inclusive future where disability is recognized as part of human diversity, and where every learner can reach their full potential.
